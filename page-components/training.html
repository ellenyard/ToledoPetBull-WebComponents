Canine Good Citizen Evaluation

$25 | Stand-alone evaluation
Has your dog mastered the basics? Sign up for an official AKC CGC evaluation — no class required.

Ready if: Your dog can reliably perform GM3/GM4-level skills with distractions.

The AKC Canine Good Citizen test evaluates 10 real-world skills that demonstrate your dog is well-mannered and under control.

  • Accepting a friendly stranger
  • Sitting politely for petting
  • Appearance and grooming
  • Walking on a loose leash
  • Walking through a crowd
  • Sit, down, and stay on cue
  • Coming when called
  • Reaction to another dog
  • Reaction to distractions
  • Supervised separation

Dogs who pass receive an official AKC CGC certificate. This is a test, not a class — come ready to show what your dog knows!
