Puppy Socialization

Set your puppy up for a lifetime of success through positive socialization and essential training.

Good Manners 2

$80 | 4-week series
Advanced training with real-world distractions. Great preparation for the Canine Good Citizen test.

Prerequisites: Completion of Good Manners 1 or trainer approval required.
  • Leash skills in real-life settings, including group walks and outdoor practice
  • Reliable recall, even around other dogs or distractions
  • Distance commands, like sit, down, and stay from afar
  • Impulse control, helping your dog resist temptation
  • Focus and engagement, so your dog looks to you even when the world is exciting
  • Distraction training, for better behavior around noise, people, and dogs
