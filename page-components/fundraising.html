So Many Ways to Give... So Many Ways to Make a Difference

Toledo's Pet Bull Project is a nonprofit 501c3 organization. It is through your generous gifts that we are able to provide services that impact our community.

All our programming falls under one of four categories: Education (teaching pet safety to young kids), Outreach (providing dog food to people that can't afford to buy any), Rescue (providing care and eventual forever homes for homeless dogs), or Operational (administrative and "behind the scenes" stuff to keep us running).

If you have a passion for a particular program, you can specify your gift to go to that area. Just click on "Add special instructions to the seller" when filling out your information on the donation form and let us know. 100% of your gift will go where you specify.

Bowling for Bullies

Strike Up Support for Dogs in Need

Saturday, May 16 at Timbers Bowling Lanes

Bowling for Bullies is back and it's one of the most fun ways to support Toledo's PET Bull Project! Join us for an unforgettable night of bowling, community, and canine cheer.

This fundraiser includes three games of bowling, shoe rental, food, a cash bar, 50/50 raffles, and a silent auction packed with great finds. Plus, don't miss the fan-favorite dog parade across the lanes, where pups steal the spotlight and remind us why we bowl for bullies in the first place.

Pricing: $35 per person • $150 for a team of five

All proceeds support programs that prevent cruelty, promote responsible pet ownership, and keep dogs safe, loved, and thriving in our community.

Whether you're forming a team with friends, bowling solo, or coming to cheer and bid, Bowling for Bullies is a feel-good night where every strike helps change lives.

Spots fill quickly—lace up your bowling shoes and join us for a night of impact, laughter, and wagging tails.

A Starry Night

Toledo's Most Paw-some Fundraising Event

Saturday, October 10 at The Pinnacle

A Starry Night is the signature fundraising event for Toledo's PET Bull Project. Join us for an unforgettable evening where dogs take center stage and compassion fuels our mission.
