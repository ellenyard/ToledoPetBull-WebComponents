So Many Ways to Give... So Many Ways to Make a Difference

Toledo's Pet Bull Project is a nonprofit 501c3 organization. It is through your generous gifts that we are able to provide services that impact our community.

All our programming falls under one of four categories: Education (teaching pet safety to young kids), Outreach (providing dog food to people that can't afford to buy any), Rescue (providing care and eventual forever homes for homeless dogs), or Operational (administrative and "behind the scenes" stuff to keep us running).

If you have a passion for a particular program, you can specify your gift to go to that area. Just click on "Add special instructions to the seller" when filling out your information on the donation form and let us know. 100% of your gift will go where you specify.

Bowling for Bullies

Strike Up Support for Dogs in Need

Saturday, May 16 at Timbers Bowling Lanes

Bowling for Bullies is back! Come out for a great night of bowling with us.

Your ticket includes three games of bowling, shoe rental, and food. There's also a cash bar, 50/50 raffles, a silent auction, and the fan-favorite dog parade across the lanes.

All proceeds go directly to programs that prevent cruelty and promote responsible pet ownership in our community.

Bring a team of friends or come on your own. Either way, it's a fun night for a good cause.

A Starry Night

Toledo's Most Paw-some Fundraising Event

Saturday, October 10 at 6:30 p.m. at The Pinnacle

A Starry Night is our biggest fundraiser of the year, and it's always a good time. Expect great food, great company, and dogs stealing the show.

The evening features our Tail Waggin' Talent Show, guest host Lissa Guyton, dinner, and a silent auction with items donated by local businesses. Every dollar raised goes to programs that prevent animal cruelty, support responsible pet ownership, and help dogs of all breeds.

Pricing: $75 per ticket • $525 for a table of eight

You can attend, sponsor as a business, or donate a silent auction item. Any way you're involved, it makes a real difference for dogs and families in Toledo.

We'd love to see you there!
