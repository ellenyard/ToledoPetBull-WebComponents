Getting Started

üêï Volunteering

As a volunteer with Toledo's PET Bull Project, you're making a direct impact on the lives of dogs in our community. Whether you're helping at events, assisting with training classes, or supporting our food pantry, every contribution matters.

  • Attend volunteer orientation (required for new volunteers)
  • Complete required training modules
  • Sign up for shifts using the calendar below
  • Contact your Team Leader with questions

üè† Fostering

Foster families are the heart of our rescue program. By opening your home, you give dogs a safe place to decompress, learn house manners, and prepare for their forever families.

  • Complete foster application and contract
  • Review foster care guidelines
  • Keep in contact with the foster coordinator
  • Report any medical concerns immediately
